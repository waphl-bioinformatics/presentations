BigBacter Bacterial genomic surveillance & clustering

Key Features

BigBacter is a bacterial genomic surveillance pipeline designed to:

  • 🦠Track bacterial isolates across runs and over time in a persistent database
  • 🦠Detect and report genomic clusters and putative transmission
  • 🦠Assemble, QC, and type isolates per organism
  • 🦠Generate surveillance-ready summaries and trees
